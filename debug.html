Debug-Chan

MP_009e.scp defines an NPC named Debug-Chan (internal name Momo) tucked away in a copy of the inn-tavern's 2F lodging room. Talking to her opens a debug menu that warps you anywhere in the scenario, flips flags, fights bosses, opens hidden maps, and grants special items. The dev gated her behind a check_game_flag(4090) 'DEBUG-build EXE check'.
